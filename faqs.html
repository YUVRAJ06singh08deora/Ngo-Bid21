Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Ideathon?

An ideathon is a short, intensive, workshop-like experience for students to address some of the most pressing challenges of our time. Participants work in teams and use design thinking and innovative learning practices to ideate and collaborate on possible solutions.

Is this Ideathon online or offline?

With this challenging covid Situation our university is still working Online so we are also conducting our Ideathon Virtually.

What if I don't have a team?

We will host a team formation event at the beginning of the Ideathon so there's no need to Ideate alone (unless you want to go solo!)

What can I Ideate about?

Anything your heart desires! or you can select any of your desired themes and give a solution about that.

What if I have never coded?

You don't have to code anything this is just an Ideathon where you can present your idea and how you will start with that.

Will you provide mentorship?

Yes we will provide mentorship forstudents who will find any kind of difficulty in Ideathon.

Anyone can attend if he is a Human Being

Yes we have a set of Rules which everyone must follow.
